the voices

this is not a page. this is a transcription.


they come at night, mostly.
sometimes around 4pm, if i'm tired.
they have things to say.
not all of it is useful.
some of it is about {{TARGET}}.

i write it down. i don't interpret.
that is someone else's job.
maybe yours.
maybe nobody's.

"the bagel was plain," they say.
yes, i tell them. we know.
they say it again anyway.
