the dream log

a running record of dreams in which {{TARGET}} has appeared. compiled without my consent by my subconscious.

2026-04-17 — the bagel
i was in line at a deli. {{TARGET}} was behind the counter. they handed me a bagel. it was plain. of course it was. i woke up sweating.

2026-04-11 — the bus
i was on a bus. so was {{TARGET}}. they were sitting one seat too close. the bus had 400 empty seats. WHY.

2026-03-30 — the door
{{TARGET}} was holding a door for me. but they were holding it slightly wrong. like 80% of the way. so i had to awkwardly reach for it. a masterclass in passive aggression. even in dreams.

2026-02-14 — valentine's day
