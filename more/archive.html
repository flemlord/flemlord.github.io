THE ARCHIVE

previous versions of flemlord.net, preserved for historical record. most are significantly worse. that's the point of an archive.

VERSIONERANOTES
v1.0 2003 – 2005 the original. comic sans. marquee everything. flame gif (lost). geocities-tier. written in notepad on a windows 98 tower.
v1.5 (lost) 2005 – 2007 added frames. then removed frames. then added them back. lost in a hard drive crash. not backed up. you're not missing much.
v2.0 (lost) 2007 – 2011 tried to use CSS. got 3 lines in. gave up. reverted to v1.0 aesthetics. this version existed for roughly 14 minutes.
v2.5 (deleted in rage) 2011 – 2014 added a blog. the blog was one post. the post was about {{TARGET}}. i deleted the whole site in an incident i don't want to discuss.
v3.0 (private) 2014 – 2019 password-protected. i am not sharing the password. i don't remember it. it is safer that way.
v3.5 (abandoned) 2019 – 2023 started a redesign. it was going to be minimalist. white background. one paragraph. it was not me. i abandoned it.
v4.0 (current) 2026 – where we are now. 20+ pages. fully cross-linked. crude on purpose. the final form. probably.

WAYBACK MACHINE
