DRAFT EULOGY

we are gathered here today to remember {{TARGET}}. a person. who lived. among us. for a while. and bothered me. considerably.

{{TARGET}} was, in many ways, a person. they had a name. they had a face. they had a preferred bagel order (plain). they had opinions they shared freely, often, and at full volume on public transit.
